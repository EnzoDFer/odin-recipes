Simple Beef Stew

Description

This is a great recipe to try your hand at a simple beef stew for those cold and wintery days. Like all stews, these can be easily modified and tailored to anyone's preference! Feel free to substitute seasonings to match the flavors you are going for.

Personally, I think the lentils are the star of the stew! They help to bring all the other flavors together and provide the base for every bite.

This recipe was inspired by Laura Bloomer Wine's Simple Beef Stew recipe. Check it out!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil: 3 tbsp
  • Beef: 1 lb cubed
  • All-purpose Flour: 1/2 cup
  • Yellow Onion: 1 minced
  • Garlic: 3 cloves minced
  • Beef Broth: 32oz
  • Crushed Tomatoes: 1 can
  • Bell Peppers: 2 chopped into strips
  • Lentils: 2 cups
  • Potatoes: 2 cubed
  • Sweet Potatoes: 2 cubed
  • Celery: 1 cup chopped
  • Carrots: 1 cup chopped
  • Salt: to taste
  • Bay leaf: 2 leaves

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat and add the flour, onion, and garlic; Stir while cooking until aromatics come out. Add meat and continue stirring occasionally for 10 minutes until browned.

  2. Step 2

    Add broth, veggies, and seasonings and let simmer for your desired length. Check meat and veggies and make sure they are as tender/soft as desired. And that's it!
