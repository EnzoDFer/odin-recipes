Chicken Salad twist

Description

This is a classic quick meal that I grew up eating all the time!

It's easy to make and only takes a few ingredients! You can also subsitute

the chicken for any choice of protein you like! You can easily make this

vegetarian or vegan by subbing out anything.

Ingredients

  • Rice: 2 cups
  • Chicken: 1 cups or to preference
  • Mayo: 2 tbsp or to preference
  • Scallions: 2 stalks
  • Salt: to taste
  • Veggies: Whatever you like!

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Prepare rice with your prepared method. I prefer a rice cooker but boiling water is A-OK!

  2. Step 2

    Prepare protein and veggies while rice is being prepared. Add to rice while mixing.

  3. Step 3

    Mix in mayo and mix until even coating throughout bowl. Top off with scallions and enjoy!
