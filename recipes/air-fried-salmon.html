Air Fried Salmon

Description

One of my all time favorites and a staple for many: Air Fried Salmon

is one of my favorite meals. It has the benefit of being flexible

and catering to a variety of styles. This is one of my favorites!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil: 2 tbsp
  • Salmon: 1 filet
  • Garlic: 3 cloves
  • Soy sauce: 2 tbsp
  • Bok Choy: 2 cups
  • Carrots: 1 cup
  • Salt: to taste
  • Pepper: to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Heat oil at medium-high heat on a pan. Pat salmon dry and season to

    taste with salt and pepper. Pre-heat air fryer to 375F.

  2. Step 2

    Add salmon to pan and begin to sear, skin side down. Pour soy sauce over

    salmon and continually spoon and pour soy sauce back on salmon for 3 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Add salmon to air fryer and allow to cook for 15 minutes by itself. Then add veggies

    to fryer and continue cooking until salmon is tender and cooked through and veggies are tender.
